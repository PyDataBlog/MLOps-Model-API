          <h2> Raspberry Pi Aspin Guide! <small>(or any singleboard computer)</small> </h2>

          <p>

            Should be simple for you guys, <br><br>

            first login and register a box on a computer or phone of your choice.
            It's very simple, you basically just click add box and it's set up in seconds.
            Once you have a box set up, go on your raspberry (if you use it) and click "Login As Box".
            Simply use your login credentials for the regular panel and login.
            Simply run that in the background and it will perform any command you see fit from your regular panel. <br><br>

            You can use Aspin whilst doing other things aswell! Obviously, you are running it in a browser. This means
            it will not interfere with any terminal applications you may want to run, no unecessary money spent on a
            new Raspberry or Tessel or whatever you may be using.
