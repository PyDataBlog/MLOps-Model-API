1. 下载anaconda

从清华大学开源软件镜像站下载anaconda, 下载地址为:[https://mirrors.tuna.tsinghua.edu.cn/anaconda/archive/Anaconda3-5.0.1-Linux-x86_64.sh](https://mirrors.tuna.tsinghua.edu.cn/anaconda/archive/Anaconda3-5.0.1-Linux-x86_64.sh)

2. 安装anaconda

运行如下命令：

> sh Anaconda3-5.0.1-Linux-x86_64.sh

出现如下选项

```
Anaconda3 will now be installed into this location:
/home/wxer/anaconda3

  - Press ENTER to confirm the location
  - Press CTRL-C to abort the installation
  - Or specify a different location below
```

按下ENTER键，则安装到/home/wxer/anaconda3目录中
