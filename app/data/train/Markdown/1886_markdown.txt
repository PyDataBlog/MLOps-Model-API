Application Performance Monitoring with Airbrake makes it easy to:
- **Understand high-level performance:** Quickly see a broad performance
  overview for your whole application.
- **Monitor user satisfaction:** Measure user satisfaction with your app
  performance using Apdex.
- **Catch problem routes:** Identify routes with slow or error-prone
  performance.
- **Analyze granular performance metrics:** Zoom into specific endpoints to see
  time spent in the DB, view, cache, external requests, and more.
- **Dive into database performance:** Analyze SQL database queries being called
  and how long they take.
- **Track your background jobs:** Monitor background job performance, track job
  failures and durations.
