Aesir
=========
[![Build Status](https://travis-ci.org/sirlag/AesirSpring.svg?branch=master)](https://travis-ci.org/sirlag/AesirSpring)


Named after the norse council of gods, Aesir is a tool designed to allow users to quickly and easily find groups to work
on time sensitive projects with.

Aesir is also designed to allow users to create their own instance of the site for local deploy, so that a game jam or a
high school teacher could use the Aesir project to organize their event.

Frameworks
=========

- [Spring.io MVC](https://spring.io/)
- [Vaadin web Framework](https://vaadin.com/home)
- [Gradle](https://gradle.org/)
