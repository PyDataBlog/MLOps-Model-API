<p>Jamendo just turned on a <a href="http://www.jamendo.com/en/creativecommons/">Creative Commons portal for browsing and searching albums by license</a> (very similar to Flickr's <a href="http://flickr.com/creativecommons">CC portal</a>).</p>
<p>While Jamendo has always been a CC music site, the portal interface makes using Jamendo extra convenient when you care about <em>which</em> CC license the music you're using falls under (e.g., for remix or commercial use).</p>
<p>If you just want to discover music you can share, Jamendo also recently rolled out <a href="http://www.jamendo.com/en/spiral/">The Spiral</a>, a convenient and visually interesting way to explore the Jamendo catalog.</p>
<p>Just imagine if Jamendo keeps <a href="http://creativecommons.org/weblog/entry/7175">adding albums</a> (now 2500+) <a href="http://creativecommons.org/weblog/entry/7191">and features</a> at this torrid pace for the remaining 10 months of 2007...</p>
